Thinker and Doer

Wendy Yeh is currently a Senior UI Engineer at The Core Group in Taipei, Taiwan. She establishes and is accountable for, the product design process that leads to shipping impactful, high-quality work—and Specializing in Design Systems. She also loves animation via code. I have over 17 years of hands-on work experience in UX design, rapid prototyping, and front-end development for rich Internet applications and mobile devices. I practice user centered design principles basing design decisions on user needs and business goals.

目前任職於金融科技公司，重新設計交易平台，從流程規劃、易用性測試、設計實作，到產品上線。在當中運用過去職涯所累積的知識，能快速構思，並提出適當的設計草圖與研發團隊討論，最後完成設計流程、設計規範文件，並交付設計視覺圖稿等與工程師協作。渴望能接觸到更多元的設計案，透過設計使世界更美好、更完善。
